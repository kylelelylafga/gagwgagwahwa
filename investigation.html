Kevin Yeardley

Kevin Yeardley has been jailed for nine years (Image: South Yorkshire Police) Sheffield paedophile Kevin Yeardley was jailed for his sick attacks on a child. Yeardley pleaded guilty to six offences, including sexual activity with a child under 16, sexual assault of a child under 13 and causing a child to engage in sexual activity. The 64-year-old's offences were described as "nothing short of horrific" by investigating officers from South Yorkshire Police. His offences were committed between 1999 and 2005 to one victim. Yeardley pleaded guilty and was sentenced to nine years in prison at Sheffield Crown Court on September 28. He has also been placed on the Sex Offenders Register.
